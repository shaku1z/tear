# C15 — Agent Academy and Demonstration Corpus

## Outcome

Historical scaffold result only. C15 established data contracts and an
in-memory majority-vote behavior-cloning prototype. It did not create a durable
real-game corpus, a trainable model, a persisted policy artifact, or a live
inference path. Operational completion is reopened by C31-C33 of the autonomous
completion plan.

## Delivered

- Canonical movement, blade, defense, enemy, boss, strategy, and interface lessons.
- Synchronized observation, action, event, reward-component, build, device, and provenance tracks.
- Review, tags, deduplication, quality scoring, and consent propagation.
- Deliberate recovery, human takeover, and policy-correction segments.
- Immutable behavior-cloning manifests and canonical encode/decode.
- Separate training, validation, calibration, and hidden release-exam splits.
- Deterministic majority behavior-cloning baseline.

## Evidence

- Every accepted sample resolved inside a valid capsule interval with explicit consent.
- Encoded/decoded observations and actions remained identical.
- Hidden release exams stayed out of ordinary exports.
- The first cloned policy passed all unseen feature seeds, including two recovery cases.

## Gate Results

- Focused Vitest: 4 tests passed.
- TypeScript: passed.
- Focused ESLint: passed.
- Source architecture: passed.

## Corrected Decision

Retain C15 as prototype evidence. Do not promote the Academy capability beyond
`prototype` until C31-C33 pass with real recorded gameplay and persisted policy
artifacts.
